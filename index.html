Everything in our world today is getting smarter. Our phones, our cars, even our homes are beoming 'smarter' to help make our lives easier. And yet, when it comes to our shopping around town, little has changed in the past decade. Sure, e-commerce and shopping on the internet has exploded, but that doesn't help with your weekly grocery shopping or trips to the nearest big box store. And there are countless shopping list apps for your smartphone out there that can serve as a digital notepad replacement. Nothing, though, has sought to really simplify your shopping experience.

With OneList, we're about to finally give day-to-day local shopping that injection of 'smart' that everything else in the world seems to have already. TheOneList is just as it sounds - its a single, unified list of things you need to buy. The power of OneList is what it can do once it knows what you need. It gives you control over your shopping trips to best fit your budget and schedule. OneList can tell you where to go to get all your items while visiting the fewest number of stores, or where to get each item for the cheapest price around. It can tell you if you are near a place that has item(s) on your list, if you'd like. Perhaps most excitingly, retailers can offer you deals directly on thing things you know you want. These features are only the beginning. OneList will soon be the only list you'll ever need.
